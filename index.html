Ludwig van Beethoven was a composer and a pianist. He remains one of the most famous and influential of all composers, this lead to him becoming a household name. He is known for his ability to compose as well as for being deaf but it is often a misconception about how long he was deaf.
A lot of people know the name and that he was deaf, but not many people know where he was born and names of his compositions.
