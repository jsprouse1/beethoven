Breif Background

Ludwig van Beethoven was a German composer and pianist. He was taught but his father Johann van Beethoven and by composer and conductor Christian Gottlob Neefe. At the age of 21 he moved to Vienna where he gained the reputation of a virtuoso pianist, he lived in Vienna until his death.
